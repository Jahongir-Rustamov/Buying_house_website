RE/MAX in the World

Founded in Denver, Colorado (USA) in 1973, RE/MAX is the largest international real estate franchising network operating in more than 85 countries.

Upon joining RE/MAX, each franchisee or associate agent becomes a member of a network composed of more than 7,000 branches and more than 130,000 associate agents

Collaboration between RE/MAX agencies allows shared businesses, where the Agent has access to clients and properties across the entire RE/MAX network, which will give you the possibility to share business with all Agents locally, nationally and internationally. This advantage allows you to close more deals than in any traditional real estate.

RE/MAX in Uzbekistan

Upon entering the Portuguese market, RE/MAX proposed to apply the innovative and successful business model worldwide and with it to change the way properties were sold and bought and a real estate agency was managed. The challenge was to drive changes in the quality of the sector - providing better customer service, introducing new management and marketing systems and professionalizing real estate agents.

The result was the rapid and consolidated growth of the RE/MAX network, which reaches more than 400 branches and includes more than 11.000 professionals. In Uzbekistan, since 2000, RE/MAX Uzbekistan has been consolidating its leadership. The strength of the brand, the efficiency of the service and the satisfaction of its employees have been recognized and awarded annually by various entities. In Portugal, RE/MAX is also the leader in the number of transactions, agencies and associated agents.

Its mission is to maintain market leadership, through professional conduct based on a strict code of ethics, with a single objective: to provide an excellent service that satisfies all of its clients' real estate needs.
